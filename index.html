About TechCERT

The Computer Emergency Response Team (TechCERT) seeks to create a formal, coalition-based structure for reducing and mitigating online threats in our community, as well as to expand technical research capacity on threats and surveillance.

Our mission is to ensure greater online freedom and security for organizations through comprehensive security services, training programs, and incident response capabilities.

We believe in the power of collaboration and knowledge sharing to build a safer digital environment for everyone.

Membership

TechCERT aims to build a coalition-based structure for preventing and mitigating online threats in our community. To achieve this goal, support services are provided through a membership model.

Membership Criteria

To become a member of TechCERT, the following criteria must be fulfilled:

  1. Organization must commit a staff member to join the TechCERT Response working group
  2. All staff of the organization must be trained on basic Internet security
  3. Organization must comply with the Traffic Light Protocol (TLP) for information sharing
  4. Active participation in community security initiatives
  5. Commitment to knowledge sharing and collaboration

Note: At present, there is no membership fee to join TechCERT. We are a community-based project and community support is essential for sustainability.

                <h3>Membership Criteria</h3>

                <p>To become a member of TechCERT, the following criteria must be fulfilled:</p>

                <ol>

                    <li>Organization must commit a staff member to join the TechCERT Response working group</li>

                    <li>All staff of the organization must be trained on basic Internet security</li>

                    <li>Organization must comply with the Traffic Light Protocol (TLP) for information sharing</li>

                    <li>Active participation in community security initiatives</li>

                    <li>Commitment to knowledge sharing and collaboration</li>

                </ol>
